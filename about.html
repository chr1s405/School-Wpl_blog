About me

Hi, I'm Christian. I'm currently studying Programming (Graduaat Programmeren) and doing an internship at Numble, a company focused on web and software development.

During this internship, I'm working on developing a webshop using Umbraco 17. This allows me to learn how professional software projects are built, tested, and maintained in a real-world environment.

As a junior developer intern, I contribute by implementing features, setting up databases and APIs, and working on the webshop backoffice.

About Numble

Numble is a digital agency specialized in building websites, web applications, and custom digital solutions. The company focuses on delivering high-quality, future-proof web projects using modern technologies and content management systems such as Umbraco.

They work with a variety of clients and help organizations design, develop, and improve their digital platforms. The focus is not only on technical implementation, but also on usability, performance, and accessibility.

My experience

During my internship, I get the opportunity to work on a real project within a professional environment. This helps me gain hands-on experience with new technologies and understand how a development team collaborates.

I'm also learning how teams work with sprint planning, code reviews, and ticket-based workflows, which gives me insight into professional development practices.

Numble operates from offices in Kontich and Ghent, where developers collaborate on various web projects and stay up to date with new developments in web technology.
