My internship with Numble

Welcome to my blog. Here I document my experiences during my internship with Numble. During this internship I am working on the development of a webshop using Umbraco 17, while learning how professional software development works in a real company environment.

My Blog

This blog documents my internship journey as I develop a webshop using Umbraco 17, reflecting on both the technical work I do and the challenges I face. It covers the tasks I tackle — like coding features, setting up APIs, and managing databases — as well as the solutions I find to problems, helping me learn and grow as a developer. By writing regularly, I can reflect on my progress, track what I’ve learned, and provide mentors and teachers with insight into my workflow, the technologies I use, and the professional development practices I follow. Ultimately, this blog serves as a personal record of my growth, showing how I evolve from learning new tools to actively contributing to a real-world project.
